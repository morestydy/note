## 1. ICE 在电生理手术中的应用

+ Catheter based， real time 2D imaging of cardic structures and blood flow 
+ US transducer is mountef on catheter tip
+ Ultrasound Catheter inserted through the femoral vein (may insert in jugular), then maneuvered into the heart
+ Typically 8-9 French diameter


+ Reliace on fluorscopy has limitations 
+ ICE allows visualization structures otherwis not seen with fluoroscopy mapping systems
+ ICE allows visualization of catheter-tissue relationships
+ Reduction and elimination of fluoroscopy further improves prodcedure
+ ICE imaging may improve procedural success outcomes


**优缺点：**
+ 电生理领域唯一一种实时成像手段
+ 对心脏组织实时成像，还能够实时观察所有的介入器械
+ 提高手术的安全性
+ 减少甚至消除术中X线依赖

+ 需要术者掌握导管技巧和影像
+ 需要额外的血管介入
+ 更多的花费
